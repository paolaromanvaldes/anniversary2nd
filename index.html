The Beginning

Once upon a time, there was a girl who met a beautiful young man in the city of love. The instant that she laid eyes on him she knew that he would be one of the most special people in her life. She watched him from afar, as she knew that the start of a foreign love would be very risky. One fateful night, her friends devised a plan to bring the girl and the man together. While she knew that if she engaged with the beautiful foreign man she would fall deeply in love with him, being near him and not speaking to him was unbearable. With some courage, she sat down next to him and nervously asked, "What's your favorite animal?"
